ABOUT US

Vault + Vine is a floral studio and thoughtfully curated retail space in East Falls, Philadelphia. Founded in 2017, we offer: Floral design services for weddings and events; Custom floral arrangements made daily and delivered; Plants for the home and garden; A collection of specialty homegoods, gifts, and objects (many sourced from Philadelphia-area creators); A café and gathering place.
